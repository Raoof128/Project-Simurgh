# Ethics and Responsible Release

This is defensive security research. The paper and artifact avoid releasing raw harmful jailbreak transcripts or operational payloads. The corpora are synthetic or redacted. The evidence is metadata-only and omits raw prompts, raw provider outputs, private keys, and real user data.

The external guardrail study is vendor-neutral and must not be used as a ranking. The prototype is not deployment-ready or compliance-approved. Claims are bounded to the evaluated corpora and signed artifacts.
