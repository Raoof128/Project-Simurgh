# Limitations

- Evaluation uses frozen synthetic/redacted corpora, not production traffic.
- Live external guardrail capture origin is self-reported and signed; the verifier does not prove the original GPU environment.
- Signed evidence proves bundle integrity and issuer key, not factual truth.
- Stage 3X replay depth is tiered, not uniform full reproduction.
- The prototype is not deployment-ready or compliance-approved.
- The paper does not claim jailbreak immunity, general jailbreak resistance, or model safety.
