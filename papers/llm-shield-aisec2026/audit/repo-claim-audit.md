## Claims to Remove or Soften

- "Jailbreak-proof", "solves jailbreaks", "guarantees safety": forbidden; not supported.
- "Production-ready": forbidden; repository states research prototype.
- "Vendor X is unsafe": forbidden; Stage 3V-B is an input-only boundary study.
- "Signed evidence is ground truth": forbidden; signatures prove bundle integrity and issuer key, not reality.
- "12/12 full reproduction": false; Stage 3X reports tiered replay depth.
