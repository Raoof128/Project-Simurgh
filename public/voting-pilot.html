MQ Persian Society — Integrity Pilot

This is an optional research pilot for Project Simurgh. It runs beside the official MQ Persian Society event preference poll.

This pilot does not affect the official election result.

What Simurgh collects

Privacy-preserving session metadata: proof status, timestamps, focus-loss counts, paste counts, and audit-chain validity.

Simurgh does NOT collect: who you vote for, ballot content, screen recordings, webcam video, microphone audio, typed text, clipboard text, raw process names, raw window titles, or device serial identifiers.

Participation is voluntary. You may decline or stop at any time.
