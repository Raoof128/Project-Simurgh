# Stage 2 macOS Risk Register

> **Status (v0.4.10, 2026-05-16):** "Stage 2 control" column items related to signed proofs, node pairing, localhost daemon, and metadata scanners are now implemented and frozen for macOS. Stage 2.6 will address Windows-specific risks. Hardware attestation remains future research.

| Risk                                          | Likelihood | Impact | Current control (macOS)                                                  | Stage 2 control status                                                    | Status     |
| --------------------------------------------- | ---------- | ------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------- |
| GPU overlays                                  | Medium     | High   | Documented limitation; scanner may catch some display-affinity cases     | Metadata-only scanner implemented; hardware attestation research required | Open       |
| Click-through overlays                        | Medium     | High   | native scanner display-affinity signals                                  | Native CoreGraphics scanner implemented and signed                        | Controlled |
| Read-only cheating                            | Medium     | High   | Documented limitation; behavioral baseline may not detect silent reading | Manual review policy and red-team calibration                             | Open       |
| Helper spoofing                               | Medium     | High   | Signed daemon proofs and Keychain-backed identity                        | P-256 signed proofs and node continuity (N1) implemented                  | Controlled |
| Session replay                                | Medium     | Medium | Sequence, timestamp, and signed proof nonce guards                       | Challenge/Response binding and nonce TTL implemented                      | Controlled |
| Invalid telemetry injection                   | Medium     | Medium | Strict schema, recursive field rejection, body limit                     | SDK/daemon envelope validation implemented                                | Controlled |
| Audit tampering                               | Low        | High   | HMAC-linked audit chain                                                  | Tamper-evident audit chain with integrity event logging                   | Controlled |
| Privacy overcollection                        | Low        | High   | Metadata-only contract, recursive field rejection, privacy audit         | Policy-hardened daemon and server-side rejection                          | Controlled |

| Lack of institutional pilot data              | High       | High   | Claims are bounded to research prototype                                 | macOS closeout docs prepared for institutional review                     | Open       |
