# Banking Shield Phase B Participant Notice

## Plain-Language Summary

You are being asked to try a research prototype using fictional banking-adjacent scenarios. The goal is to check whether the consent, warning, withdrawal, report, audit, and verification flow is understandable.

This is not a real banking tool.

## What You Will Do

You will:

- Read this notice.
- Confirm that you understand the prototype is fictional.
- Use the existing Banking Shield demo pages.
- Complete five fictional scenarios.
- Open report, audit, and verify outputs.
- Try one withdrawal path.
- Complete a short feedback form.

## What You Must Not Enter

Do not enter real:

- Account numbers.
- BSBs.
- Card numbers.
- OTPs.
- Credentials or login IDs.
- Balances.
- Payees.
- Transaction amounts.
- Payment references.
- Transaction descriptions.
- Bank names tied to your own life.
- Screenshots or screen recordings.
- App names, process names, or window titles.
- Personal finance details.

Use only the fixed fictional categories already shown by the prototype.

## What This Prototype Does Not Do

The prototype does not:

- Connect to banks.
- Process payments.
- Check real payees.
- Access CDR.
- Detect fraud.
- Prevent scams.
- Provide financial advice.
- Assess reimbursement.
- Claim APRA, CDR, AML, or Confirmation of Payee compliance.
- Inspect your real device, screen, browser windows, or banking apps.

## Data Handling

The dry run records aggregate-only results. It is intended to keep no real banking data and no per-tester sensitive banking content.

Reports should show all sensitive collection assertions as `false`.

## Withdrawal

You may stop at any time. The prototype includes a withdrawal path. A withdrawn session should not export a report, while audit and verify exports may remain available for transparency.

## Confirmation

Before participating, confirm:

- [ ] I understand this is a fictional internal dry run.
- [ ] I will not enter real banking data.
- [ ] I understand this is not fraud detection or financial advice.
- [ ] I understand reports are prototype evidence only.
- [ ] I understand I may stop or withdraw.
