## Purpose

This document requests written approval from the MQ Persian Society executive committee to proceed with Phase C of the Simurgh Voting Pilot: an optional, voluntary pilot with real society members alongside an official society ballot.

## What is being requested

Permission to invite volunteer society members to optionally participate in the Simurgh privacy-preserving session integrity pilot during a society election. Participation is entirely voluntary and does not affect any member's vote.

## What Simurgh does

Simurgh records privacy-preserving session metadata — proof status, timestamps, and audit-chain validity — without collecting ballot choice, candidate selection, or any personally identifying information.

Members who decline or withdraw are treated identically to members who do not participate at all.

## What Simurgh does NOT do

- It does not record who anyone votes for
- It does not affect the official ballot or election result
- It does not identify or surveil individual voters
- It does not produce results used for disciplinary action
- It is not a vote validator or election security system
- It is not a misconduct detection tool
